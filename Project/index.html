ABOUT US

Established in 1989, B's Barbers has grown to a large network of branches nationwide. With its stylish, modern interiors, and commitment to providing excellent customer service, it has become the go-to place for men’s grooming in the Philippines.

For men in need of pampering, Barbershop is a full service barbershop that provides an environment where men can relax and come out feeling good about themselves.

Dedicated to bringing the best out of our clients, B's Barbers stands out from the traditional barbershop experience and goes beyond its services to create the perfect ambience with well-trained barbers and therapists to cater to our clients’ needs.

We’ve been providing the traditional barbershop experience by ­setting a venue where dudes can relax, and get groomed. Our approach is offering simple and straightforward services that are delivered well, and a vibe that is curated for the discerning man.

We believe that the way to become a Better Dude is to be specific in everything that he does. That's why the tools, products, and processes were carefully reviewed to ensure you are groomed the right way.

This is complemented by our curated playlists, with selections from jazz, blues, rock, and house, and our beverage offerings, ­handbrewed coffee and craft beer.
